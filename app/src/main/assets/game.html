1. Level Experience

The level system uses a progressive experience model. Max Level: 60.

Exp = floor(100 + 10*(L-1) + 0.4*(L-1)^2)
  • Early levels are fast (Lvl 1→2 needs 100 Exp).
  • Total Exp to Max Level (60) is approx 62,000.

3. Economic Model

Land Unlock

  • Level 1: 1 Land
  • Every 5 Levels: +1 Land
  • Level 55: Max 12 Lands (All Unlocked)

Profit & Yield

P = (C * (1 + 0.12 * T)) / Y
  • P: Base Sell Price per unit
  • C: Seed Cost (Price)
  • T: Harvest Time (Hours)
  • Y: Yield Quantity
  • Exp: 1 Exp per 5 Coins spent.
