# Seeker Farm - Gameplay & Strategy Guide

## 🎮 Core Gameplay Loop

Seeker Farm is an isometric farming simulation where you build your agricultural empire on the Solana blockchain.

1.  **Plant**: Purchase seeds from the Shop and plant them on your available land tiles.
2.  **Cultivate**: Water your crops to ensure they grow efficiently.
3.  **Harvest**: Once crops are ripe, harvest them to collect produce.
4.  **Trade**: Sell your harvested crops for Coins to buy better seeds and expand your farm.

## 📈 Upgrade & Progression Strategy

### 1. Land Expansion (Leveling Up)
Your farm starts with a single plot of land. To maximize production, you must unlock more tiles by increasing your **Farmer Level**.

-   **Unlock Formula**: New Land = `Level 1 + (Tile Index * 5)`
-   **Milestones**:
    -   **Level 1**: Start with 1 Tile.
    -   **Level 6**: Unlock 2nd Tile.
    -   **Level 11**: Unlock 3rd Tile.
    -   ...and so on.
-   **Strategy**: Focus on completing tasks and harvesting crops to gain XP and level up quickly. Every 5 levels grants you a significant boost in production capacity.

### 2. Crop Economy & Profit
Different crops suit different playstyles. Choose wisely based on your activity:

| Crop Type | Duration | Best For | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Short** (< 6h) | Fast | Active Players | Highest profit per hour. Grind these when you are online to accumulate coins quickly. |
| **Mid** (6-12h) | Medium | Casual Play | Good balance of profit and time. Perfect for checking in twice a day. |
| **Long** (12-24h) | Slow | Overnight | Plant these before sleeping to maximize downtime efficiency. |
| **Special** (24h+) | Very Slow | Long-term | High-value crops for massive payouts after long waits. |

**Profit Formula**: `Price = Cost * (1 + 0.12 * Time) / Yield`
*Longer-growth crops generally offer better ROI per seed, but short crops offer better ROI per hour if harvested instantly.*

### 3. Resource Management
-   **Coins**: Your primary currency. Reinvest 80% of your profits into buying more seeds to keep your land productive.
-   **Water**: Keep your soil wet! Watered crops grow faster and are less likely to wither.
-   **Solana Integration**: Connect your wallet to secure your assets and participate in the on-chain economy.
